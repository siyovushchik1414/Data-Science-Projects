The model has been presented by Hamidov Siyovush in the Republican contest "Student and technological progress" organized by The Ministry of Education of The Rupublic of Tajikistan in 2023.
Data represents a great amount of x-ray pictures. The model classifies whether a given x-ray of a 0 to six years old child is being ill.

The Model's Pipeline:
Data engineering
Exploratory Data Analysis
Data Normalizing
Data Augmentation
Model Training using CNN

The result is 90% of accuracy.
